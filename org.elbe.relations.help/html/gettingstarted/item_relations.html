Displaying the item's relations

Displaying the item's relations means displaying the item in its context. The view capable of doing this is the Relations browser. The aim of the Relations browser is to give you an impression what connections an item has.

The following actions display an item and its relations in the Relations browser:

  • Double click an item in one of the selection views.
  • Double click an item in the search view.
  • Select a related item in the Relations browser and make it the center item.

A newly created item will be displayed in the Relations browser by default, together with it's relations.
