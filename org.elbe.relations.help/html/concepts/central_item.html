The central item

The central item is the item at the center of the Relations browser, thus you can see all relations of the central item.

There are various possibilities to show an item as central item:

  • Display the context menu on a related item and select the menu entry Center selected item. This makes this item the central item.
  • If an item different from the central item is the active item, you can press Ctrl+C. This makes the active item the central item.
  • Pressing the Ctrl key while moving the mouse cursor on related items in the Relations browser renders the related item's title clickable. Then, if you click this item, it will moved to the Relations browsers center and display its relations.
  • Double clicking an item in one of the selection views or the search view will display this item as central item in the Relations browser.
  • If you select an item in one of the selection views or the search view (using either the mouse pointer or the arrow keys) and then pressing the Enter key will display the selected item as central item in the Relations browser.
