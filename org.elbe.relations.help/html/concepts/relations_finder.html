The Relations finder

The Relations application provides an additional browser view to display an item's relations context: the Relations finder.

This view displays the central item in the left pane and its related items in the right pane. The example shows the central term item "physics" in relation with seven other items (three term items, two text items and two person items).

You can switch the focus from one pane to the other using the Tab key.

If you are in the left pane, pressing the Enter key will set the focus to the right pane too. If you are in the right pane, however, pressing the Enter key will make the selected item central, i.e. move it to the left pane and display its relations in the right pane.

If you are in the right pane, you can move the selection marker up and down either using the Up Arrow and Down Arrow respectively or by entering the beginning characters of the term you want the selection marker to move to.

The work on the items displayed in the browser view, you have exactely the same possibilities as in the classic Relations browser (e.g. context menu and shortcuts).
