The Inspector view

The Inspector view displays the content of the item having the focus in the relations browser.

A Term item in the inspector.

A Text item's bibliography in the inspector.

A Text item's text field in the inspector.

This view allows rapid inspection and quick edit of the active (i.e. focused) item's content. The extent of the editable information in this view is dependent on the item's type.
A Term item makes both the item's title and text editable. In the case of a Person item, it's the additional text that can be edited whereas the persons "title" (name, firstname) is readonly.
If you have a Text item in the inspector, the item's title is editable in any case. In the inspector's content field you can switch between the bibliographical display of the term (in read only mode) or an editable view or the item's text field. In this mode, you can quickly edit the content of this field.
You can switch the dispaly mode using the view menu.
